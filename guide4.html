Resident Evil Village: Survival Tips

Resident Evil Village is a terrifying survival horror game that requires careful resource management, puzzle-solving, and combat strategy. In this guide, we’ll share the best tips to help you survive the horrors lurking in the village.

Combat and Ammo Management

Learn how to conserve your ammunition, prioritize which enemies to engage, and maximize the use of your weapons for different situations.

Exploring the Village

Find hidden items, unlock doors, and discover secret passages that will help you progress through the game’s environment.

Solving Puzzles

Master the puzzle mechanics of Resident Evil Village, from finding key items to solving intricate challenges to unlock new areas.
