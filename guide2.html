Top Tips for Playing Horizon Forbidden West

Horizon Forbidden West is a visually stunning open-world game, but to truly master the game, you need a few expert tips. This guide will give you insights on how to make the most of your time in the world of the Forbidden West, from combat to resource management.

Combat Mastery

Get familiar with the different weapons and combat styles, and learn how to effectively counter the various mechanical beasts you encounter.

Resource Management

Discover how to gather and manage the vast array of resources in the game to craft essential items, from ammo to health potions.

Exploration and Side Quests

Explore the map in depth and uncover hidden treasures. Side quests can unlock new skills, weapons, and even lore that enriches your journey.
