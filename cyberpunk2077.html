Cyberpunk 2077: Phantom Liberty Review

★★★★☆

Cyberpunk 2077: Phantom Liberty offers a more refined experience with exciting new characters, expanded stories, and improved mechanics. While the base game had its issues, this expansion truly shines in making the world more immersive and dynamic. The narrative depth is richer, and the mechanics feel more polished, giving players a much better experience than the original release.

Pros:

  • Expanded story and new characters
  • Refined mechanics and visuals
  • Improved pacing

Cons:

  • Still some technical issues in certain areas
  • Can feel repetitive at times
