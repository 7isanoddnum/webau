Starfield Review

★★★☆☆

Starfield is an ambitious RPG with an expansive universe, but its execution falls short in certain areas. The vastness of space is impressive, but the pacing feels slow at times, and the exploration mechanics aren't as engaging as expected. There's a lot of potential here, but it doesn't fully live up to the hype.

Pros:

  • Massive universe with great scope
  • Solid combat mechanics
  • Stunning visuals

Cons:

  • Pacing issues and slow exploration
  • Storytelling could be more engaging
