Resident Evil Village Review

★★★★☆

Resident Evil Village takes everything great about its predecessor and amplifies it with a fantastic new setting, terrifying enemies, and an unforgettable story. The survival-horror aspects are paired with intense action sequences, providing a mix of thrills that keep players on the edge of their seat. Though not without a few predictable moments, the game is a massive success for the franchise.

Pros:

  • Great atmosphere and visual design
  • Engaging and suspenseful gameplay
  • Solid combat mechanics

Cons:

  • Some predictable plot twists
  • Occasionally repetitive enemy encounters
