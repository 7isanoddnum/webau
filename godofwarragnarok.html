God of War Ragnarok Review

★★★★★

God of War Ragnarok is the epic conclusion to Kratos and Atreus’ journey. The game takes everything great about its predecessor and expands upon it, delivering an emotional and visually stunning experience. The characters are more developed, and the combat feels smoother, making this a must-play for fans of action games and narratives alike.

Pros:

  • Incredible story and character development
  • Beautiful graphics and fluid combat
  • Epic boss fights and gameplay

Cons:

  • Some linearity in gameplay progression
  • Minor pacing issues in certain story sections
