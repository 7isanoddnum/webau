Horizon Forbidden West Review

★★★★☆

Horizon Forbidden West builds on the foundations set by its predecessor, delivering an expansive world filled with stunning visuals, engaging combat, and a rich narrative. The protagonist, Aloy, embarks on a journey to uncover the mysteries of the Forbidden West while dealing with both new and familiar threats. This game improves in almost every aspect compared to Zero Dawn.

Pros:

  • Stunning visuals and open world
  • Engaging story with interesting characters
  • Improved combat mechanics

Cons:

  • Some minor technical issues and glitches
  • Side missions can feel repetitive
